1) Briefly describe the artifact. What is it? When was it created?

The artifact is an enhanced Binary Search Tree (BST) with integrated Doubly Linked List (DLL) functionality. The BST allows efficient insertion, deletion, and searching of bid data, while the DLL maintains the order of bids as they are inserted, providing an alternative way to display and traverse the bids. The initial project was created in Fall of 2023 as part of a coursework exercise to demonstrate advanced data structure implementation and manipulation in C++.
